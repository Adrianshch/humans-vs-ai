    npm install  then  npm start
  2. Open http://localhost:3000 in your browser.

Don't double-click index.html — the game needs the running server for styling, buttons, and the AI players.

🌱 Ethics & Energy Transparency

This game's whole premise is turning AI cost into something you can feel. So we don't hide it.

Where the AI runs

  • No data centers, no cloud LLM calls. The AI players run on a small model via Ollama on the machine hosting this game.
  • Tiny model on purpose. Default is a ~1B-parameter model — orders of magnitude cheaper than a frontier chatbot. It sips power like a video game, not a render farm.
  • No per-query water/cooling cost the way hyperscale data centers incur. The only energy is the electricity your own laptop already draws.
  • Learning is free. Bots improve GAN-style from your votes — no expensive retraining runs; the "training" is you playing.

How it learns (the GAN loop)

You, the human voters, are the discriminator. Every round:

  • Real human answers are saved as the distribution the bots imitate.
  • AI answers that survive a vote are reinforced. Answers caught as AI are penalized.
  • This corpus conditions future answers, so bots get harder to detect over time — and it's exported as a preference dataset for real offline fine-tuning.

Footprint goal

Target: no heavier per-player than Jackbox, Kahoot, Among Us, or a YouTube tab. A short local generation is a fraction of a second of GPU/CPU — comparable to a few seconds of video playback.
